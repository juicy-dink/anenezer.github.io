welcome to Cyber Talent

This cyber talent development center was established by the Information Network Security Administration to enable our country, Ethiopia, to be competitive in the field by producing the next generation of cyber security experts. The Cyber Talent Center is a dynamic hub committed to nurturing and developing the next generation of cybersecurity experts. With a focus on education, training, and innovation, the center serves as a catalyst for fostering skills that are essential in safeguarding digital landscapes against evolving cyber threats.
